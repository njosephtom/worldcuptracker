Brazil at the World Cup — History

Brazil have won the FIFA World Cup a record five times: 1958, 1962, 1970, 1994, and 2002. They are the only nation to have qualified for every World Cup since 1930 (22 consecutive tournaments). The 2026 World Cup is Brazil's chance at a long-awaited sixth title — their last win was 24 years ago in Japan/South Korea 2002.
