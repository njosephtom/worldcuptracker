England at the FIFA World Cup — History

England won the FIFA World Cup once, in 1966, as hosts at Wembley Stadium. They have appeared in 16 World Cups and reached the semi-finals in 1990 and the quarter-finals multiple times since. At the 2018 World Cup they reached the semi-finals; at Euro 2020 and Euro 2024 they reached the final.

The 2026 World Cup, with Jude Bellingham and Bukayo Saka leading a golden generation, represents England's best shot at a second World Cup in 60 years.
