About WorldCupTracker.us

worldcuptracker.us  ·  Independent FIFA World Cup 2026 Tracker

Our mission: Provide football fans with a free, fast, and reliable way to follow every match of the 2026 FIFA World Cup — no login, no paywall, no clutter.

What We Do

WorldCupTracker.us is a free, real-time FIFA World Cup 2026 tracker built for fans who want instant access to live scores, the complete match schedule, group stage standings, the knockout bracket, squad rosters, and venue information — all in one place.

Our tracker covers all 104 matches across the 48-team tournament hosted in the United States, Canada, and Mexico from June 11 to July 19, 2026.

Key Features

  • Live Scores — Real-time match updates with goals, cards, and match events
  • Full Schedule & Fixtures — Every group stage and knockout match with local timezone support
  • Group Standings — Auto-updating points tables for all 12 groups
  • Knockout Bracket — Visual bracket from Round of 32 to the Final
  • Squad Information — Rosters and FIFA rankings for all 48 teams
  • Multi-Timezone Support — Toggle between ET, CT, MT, PT, or use your local time
  • Dark & Light Mode — Choose the theme that works best for you
  • Mobile Responsive — Optimized for phones, tablets, and desktops
  • No Account Required — Completely free with no registration needed

Who We Are

WorldCupTracker.us is an independent project created by a small team of football fans and web developers. We built this tracker because we wanted a clean, fast, and ad-light experience for following the World Cup — something we couldn't find elsewhere.

We are not affiliated with FIFA, any national football association, or any official tournament organizer. All match data is sourced from publicly available sports data feeds.

Why We Built This

The 2026 FIFA World Cup is the biggest edition in history — 48 teams, 104 matches, 16 stadiums across three countries. Existing trackers are often cluttered with pop-ups, require accounts, or bury the information fans actually need behind paywalls.

We wanted something simple: open a page, see today's matches, check the standings, follow the bracket. That's WorldCupTracker.us.

How It Works

The site is a lightweight web application that fetches live match data and displays it in real time. It auto-refreshes every 5 minutes during live matches, supports multiple timezone displays, and works entirely in your browser with no downloads or installations.
